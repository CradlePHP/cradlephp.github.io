Sink - Controllers

Controllers can be found in both app/api/src/controller and app/www/src/controller. They interact with both the templates and jobs exclusively. Controllers utilize the route construct and follow a common process.

  1. Validate access
  2. Prepare Data
  3. Trigger jobs
  4. Interpret Response from Jobs
  5. Redirects, calls another route or renders the response content

Controllers can be separated into several app folders optionally pairing with a template folder where applicable depending on your preference of responsibilities.
